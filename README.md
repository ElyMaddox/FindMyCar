# FindMyCar

**FindMyCar** is an all-in-one platform that helps users search for, compare, and evaluate vehicles while preparing for a purchase.

## Personalized Vehicle Search

Users can create a profile to specify their preferences, including:

- **Preferred makes** (e.g., Toyota, Ford)
- **Vehicle types** (e.g., sedan, SUV, truck)
- **Desired features** (e.g., AWD, Blind-Spot Monitoring)

The platform filters search results and recommendations based on these preferences, excluding vehicles that do not meet the user’s criteria.

To support users who may not be familiar with the automotive market, FindMyCar offers:

- **Pre-set preference profiles**
- An integrated **AI assistant** to guide profile setup and answer vehicle-related questions

---

## AI-Powered Assistance

The built-in AI assistant helps users:

- Explore makes, models, and trims
- Receive personalized recommendations
- Understand feature differences
- Evaluate fair pricing for new and used vehicles

By leveraging profile data, the assistant delivers customized advice tailored to each user’s needs.

---

## Local Listings & Smart Filtering

FindMyCar integrates GPS functionality to help users:

- Locate dealerships nearby
- Search available inventory in their area
- Filter listings based on price, features, and preferences

Each listing clearly displays pricing and feature details, ensuring transparency in the decision-making process.

---

## Side-by-Side Comparison

Users can compare two vehicles directly to:

- Highlight feature differences
- Evaluate pricing
- Identify the best fit for their needs
